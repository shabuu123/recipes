kheer

Today, we make kheer in easy way.

Indigredients to make kheer

  • Basmati rice
  • Whole milk
  • Condensed milk
  • Some dry fruits
  • cardamom powder

Process to make kheer

  1. First soak 1/4 cup raw rice in water till immersing level. You can use basmati rice instead of raw rice. Soak in water for 30 mins.
  2. Rinse rice 2-3 times. Strain and set aside.
  3. In a large pot- add 4 cups milk that is 1 litre milk.
  4. Let it come to a rolling boil.
  5. Simmer for 5 mins, meanwhile keep stirring and scraping the sides to collect the malai and add it to the boiling milk itself.
  6. Now add soaked, drained rice to the milk. Give a quick mix.
  7. Cook until rice turns soft.
  8. Now add 1/4 cup sugar.
  9. Add 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder and few strands of saffron.
  10. Mash the rice slightly with a ladle.
  11. Add 1 tablespoon chopped mixed nuts.
  12. Switch off when it is still slightly runny as it thickens with time. Rice kheer is ready to serve.
