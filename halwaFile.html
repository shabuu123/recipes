Halwa recipe

Today, we made Gajar ka Halwa in easy way.

Ingredients to make halwa recipe

  • Carrots
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Some dry fruits
  • Milk

Process to make Gajar ka Halwa

  1. Firstly, To a pan heat 1 tablespoon ghee add 1 tablespoon chopped nuts.
  2. Fry until golden. Remove to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Wash carrots well, trim the edges and peel off the skin. Grate it using a grater,measure 2cups and add it.
  4. Saute for at least 3-5 mins or until it shrinks a bit and the color changes.
  5. Add 1 and 1/2 cups full fat milk (boiled).
  6. Keep the flame in low medium for the milk to reduce.
  7. It will nicely bubble and start reducing slowly. Stir on and off. Cook for 10 mins and until milk reduce.
  8. keep cooking in medium flame.
  9. Add 1/3 cup sugar.
  10. It will start to thicken.
  11. Give a quick stir.
  12. Add fried nuts.
  13. Give a quick stir and switch off.
