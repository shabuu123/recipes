Aalu paratha

This is very delicious and tasty Aalu paratha dish.

Indigredient to make Aalu paratha

  • Potatoes
  • Chili
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • masala

Process to make Aalu paratha

  1. Boil the potatoes on high flame until they are cooked.
  2. Fry the potatoes in oil until their color turns brown
  3. We will knead the flour and melt it and then put potatoes in the flour.
  4. After this make the dough like a roti and boke it for 5 minutes.
  5. Now our Aalu paratha is ready.
