Chilli Panner

Today, we made Chilli Panner in easy way.

Ingredients to make Chilli Paneer recipe

  • Paneer
  • Flour
  • Sauces
  • Vegetables
  • Spice Powders

Process to make Chilli Panner

  1. Take 200 grams paneer rinse it well first.
  2. Cut into small cubes.
  3. Repeat to finish and set aside.
  4. To a small bowl add 1 teaspoon soya sauce, 1 and 1/2 teaspoon chilli sauce. 2 tablespoon tomato sauce, and other powders.
  5. Mix well and set aside.
  6. To another small bowl add 1 heaped teaspoon cornflour with 2 tablespoon water, mix it well to form a slurry and set aside.
  7. In a mixing bowl add 1/4 cup cornflour and 1/4 cup maida along with 1/2 teaspoon pepper powder.
  8. Mix it well and set aside.
  9. Add water little by little to form a slightly thick batter.
  10. The batter should be flowing but thick to coat the paneer.
  11. Heat oil in a kadai add the coated panner cubes few at a time depending upon the kadai size.
  12. Flip over to fry it even on all sides.
  13. Fry until it turns golden brown on all sides and is crisp.
  14. Repeat to finish. Remove the paneer cubes and drain in a tissue paper. Break the pieces if it sticks to each other. Set aside.
  15. Add 1/2 cup onion, 1/2 cup capsicum.
  16. Saute for a minute. Then add required salt along with 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/2 teaspoon pepper powder.
  17. saute for few seconds.
  18. Add prepared sauce mixture.
  19. Give a quick mix to the cornflour slurry as the flour might settle at the bottom. Now add prepared cornflour slurr along with 1/2 teaspoon rice vinegar.
  20. Give a quick mix.
  21. Cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
  22. Add fried paneer cubes.
  23. Toss well and add 1 tablespoon spring onion.
  24. Toss well and switch off.
  25. Chilli Paneer is ready! Serve hot!.
